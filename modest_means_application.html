How pro bono and modest means work

California Lawyers for the Arts can make referrals for pro bono and modest means clients meeting certain financial criteria. If an applicant does not qualify for pro bono assistance after reviewing the financial screening application, the client may qualify for one of three tiers within our modest means program.
